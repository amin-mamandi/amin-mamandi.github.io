About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of Kansas, working with Prof. Mohammad Alian. My research revolves around computer architecture and computer networks, with a primary objective of unlocking greater computational capabilities within dependable hardware. This extends to both specialized applications and broader, general-purpose computing. My current research is centered on in-memory and near-memory computing, particularly in the context of data centers. You can find my CV!
